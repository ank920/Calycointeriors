# Calyco Interiors

Calyco Interiors is a city-by-city interiors execution business built under the Calyco brand.

The goal is to provide reliable, professional, and transparent interior execution services for homes, offices, and commercial spaces through verified local operating partners, contractors, vendors, and supervisors.

---

## 1. Business Overview

Calyco Interiors is not only an interior design service. It is an execution-focused interiors platform.

We help customers with:

- Full home interiors
- Modular kitchens
- Wardrobes and storage
- Painting and wall finishes
- Waterproofing and surface repair
- Electrical and plumbing work
- Carpentry and civil work
- False ceiling and lighting
- Office and commercial fit-outs
- Renovation and repair projects

The model is designed to combine:

- Local city execution
- Central brand support
- Standardized quotation systems
- Vendor and contractor networks
- Customer-facing trust and accountability

## 6. Customer Workflow

### Step 1: Lead Capture

Customer enquiry comes from:

- Website
- WhatsApp
- Phone call
- Referral
- Builder/broker/designer network
- Social media
- Local business development

Basic information collected:

- Name
- Phone number
- Location
- Property type
- BHK / area
- Type of work required
- Budget range
- Timeline
- Photos/videos of site, if available

---

### Step 2: Initial Assessment

The team reviews:

- Customer requirement
- Site photos/videos
- Approximate scope
- Budget suitability
- Urgency
- Location feasibility

A rough estimate may be shared based on photos before a site visit.

---

### Step 3: Site Visit

A site visit is scheduled for qualified customers.

During the visit:

- Measurements are taken
- Scope is clarified
- Material preferences are discussed
- Site constraints are noted
- Customer expectations are documented

---

### Step 4: BOQ and Quotation

A written BOQ and quotation are prepared.

Quotation should include:

- Scope of work
- Item-wise pricing
- Material specifications
- Exclusions
- Timeline
- Payment milestones
- Warranty terms
- GST details, if applicable

---

### Step 5: Project Confirmation

Project starts only after:

- Customer approves quotation
- Advance payment is received
- Timeline is agreed
- Material/vendor plan is finalized

---

### Step 6: Execution

Execution includes:

- Vendor coordination
- Contractor allocation
- Material procurement
- Daily site supervision
- Customer updates
- Quality checks
- Issue resolution

---

### Step 7: Handover

Before handover:

- Final inspection is done
- Snag list is prepared
- Pending items are closed
- Customer approval is taken
- Final payment is collected

---

### Step 8: After-Service

Post-handover support includes:

- Warranty tracking
- Complaint handling
- Customer review collection
- Referral request
- Maintenance support, where applicable

## 8. City Partner Compensation Model

The City Operating Partner model may include:

- Small recoverable monthly draw
- Profit-share linked to collected project profit
- Performance-based step-up
- Quality holdback
- Warranty reserve
- Monthly reconciliation

Example structure:

- Monthly recoverable draw: ₹35,000
- Profit share: 25% of net profit initially
- Step-up possible to 30% or 33% based on performance
- Profit calculated after project costs, employee/consultant costs, and agreed city cost allocation
- Payout only after customer collection

This keeps the model entrepreneurial and performance-driven.
